About the Project

Sooooooo, I started this project because I wanted to show off some of the skills I have that I do not always know how to articulate. I was also let go from my last job at a weird time in my development as a programmer. I was just getting to a point where I was semi comfortable with my skills and I was working on an awesome project that was really pushing me to learn more. I had a mentor in a sense, someone I could ask questions to when I was stuck. I really felt like I was getting traction and my boss used me as a scapegoat for his own inabilities and I was let go. It really sucked because I was getting better at what I was doing but it really affected my confidence. Not only that but I really didn't know what direction I was heading in the first place. So I did what I always do, continued taking classes online to learn more so I could feel more confident. The classes were not helping so I decided I needed a project. I had just missed a job opportunity because I did not know enough django. So why not work with django some more. I had a taste of it so let's continue building on that. It was perfect, I could follow some tutorials to get started and then build from there. Not only that but I would be challenged in many different areas, python, web, web hosting, ssl, domains, the cloud, and git. I had some experience in all of them but I have never had to bring them all together. I will continue to post and add updates to the project as I go but I figure this will atleast give you an idea of what you are looking at here. Thank you and hope you come back to see how it goes.
